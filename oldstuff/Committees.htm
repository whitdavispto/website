Fall Festival

Special Events

Co-Chairs: Shannah Montgomery, Ani Fischer

This committee is in charge of planning and organizing the movie and skate nights throughout the school year. Movie nights are specifically aimed at Pre-K through 2nd graders. "G" rated movies are selected and the committee solicits volunteers to help pop popcorn for sale along with drinks and other snacks. Skate nights target the older students, grades 3rd - 5th, although many of the older students' younger siblings often attend. Skate nights are held at Skatearound USA located in front of the Satterfield Ball Park. Admission is $3 with a $2 skate rental fee. PTO receives 50% of the admission and rental fee. Food and drinks are available for a nominal fee from Skatearound's concession stand.

Staff Appreciation

Co-Chairs: Sabrina Young

A monthly celebration of birthdays. Each month students within an assigned grade (PTO will notify students with a flier in their PAWS folder) are asked to bring in a snack, appetizer, dessert, or other favorite food for Whit Davis teachers and staff to enjoy.
