   Coupon Book Sales
   Co-Chairpersons:  Dana Turner, Sharyn Dickerson     

Fantastic Savings Books. This is PTO's most successful fundraiser of the year.

The sale begins August 22nd and ends on Tuesday, September 6th. Books sell for $12 each. PTO gets $6 for every book sold. In 2010, students sold ~$26,000 in books and donations. As a result PTO raised ~ $13,000 for our school!
